Nevada County Arts Council acknowledges that the original inhabitants of territories
now known as Nevada County continue to be home to indigenous peoples. Generations
of Washoe and Nisenan tribal communities, east and west, shared boundaries with
Maidu peoples and developed deep understandings of these lands. Through ceremony,
culture and stewardship our indigenous peoples continue long standing relationships
with the land, as well as with water, air, plant and animal beings. We are committed
to understanding that the voices of our indigenous communities are primary voices,
and to supporting their leadership towards a more just future for everyone.
