Aligning Truckee Cultural District’s support of our creative
communities to a new brand, and consulting with long-time locals,
here are our favorite spots to dine, enjoy the outdoors, have
adventures, relax, work while on vacation, party, play, and connect.
Like many tourism-based communities, locals enjoy these spots
in the off-season, when we can easily find parking, and have a more
intimate experience downtown, or in nature, with our neighbors,
and with friends and family who visit for the fresh mountain air.

Wine with a little funk
Visit Truckee River Winery for an exclusive high altitude single
vineyard wine experience, where nature's artistry and bold flavors
collide. It’s family-run, with passionate craftsmanship, and award-
winning wines from Sierra Foothills vineyards. Here you will find
backyard tasting menu events, including charcuterie boards,
bocce courts, and seasonal barn mixers with BBQs.
Meanwhile, The Pour House is known as “the funkiest wine and
cheese shop in downtown Truckee,” specializing in smaller
production wines from around the world with prices affordable
for locals. Sip, savor, and snack on a regionally sourced and locally
