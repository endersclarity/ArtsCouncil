                                                                   Our 2026 issue of MUSE introduces a new annual

                                                                  bring ideas and experiences to life—and, through
                                                                     this, to best define our sense of place. All our
                                                                  stories in MUSE grow from this idea, but our Story
                                                                   of Place feature will have placemaking at its core.

                                                                        Nevada County is woven from the threads of

                                                                       upon the resilience of our peoples, the beauty
                                                                          and fragility of our landscapes, and the

                                                                                both surround and bind us.
