The founder of Nevada City Winery grew up in Nevada City, but
traveled and studied in Europe, where he found himself enamored
with winemaking. He, too, was convinced Nevada County offered
the soils and climate to “make wines that challenge the world’s
best.” One of the very first established wineries in the county,
Nevada City Winery is now women-owned and operated.
Family-owned Lucchesi Vineyards has been producing wine on its
steeply terraced, 20-acre property nestled in the Sierra Foothills
for 25 years. Their handcrafted wines can be sampled at their
winery with a reservation, and any time at their tasting room,
located in the heart of downtown Grass Valley.
From the snow-covered vineyards of Truckee Family Wines in
Eastern Nevada County, to the rolling hills of Naggiar in South
County, wine enthusiasts will discover unique Italian grapes,        A QUIET REVOLUTION
Hungarian style blends, and cold weather vines—all within
a short, scenic drive.
