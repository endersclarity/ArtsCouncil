The Truckee Arts Alliance is excited to announce Carrie Haines
as its new Program Coordinator. Haines brings a wealth
of experience with her to the new role. From her work with
the Truckee Community Theater as Artistic Director and
Director of Education, her passion for the arts and community
engagement makes her a perfect fit. “Carrie is deeply
invested in our Truckee arts community, is multi-talented
and has contributed significantly to the trajectory of our
local performing arts scene. She has a strong vision for
positioning Truckee as an arts destination working in concert
with Truckee creatives in all disciplines,” says Kellie Cutler,
Program Manager of Truckee Cultural District. Truckee Arts
Alliance 2025 programs include a first ever Truckee Arts
Alliance Town Hall, Truckee Talent Show and Truckee
Arts Festival.
