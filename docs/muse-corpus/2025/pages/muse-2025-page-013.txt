                                                                 Trails & Vistas Celebrates Twenty
                                                                 Years of Art Hikes Within Nature
                                                                 Since 2004, Trails & Vistas has brought a diverse assemblage
                                                                 of arts and culture to the rural mountains of eastern Nevada
                                                                 County. Their Art Hikes showcase visual and performance
                                                                 art including music, poetry, dance and earthworks. Founder
                                                                 and Artistic Director Nancy Tieken Lopez established the
                                                                 nonprofit’s Art Hikes, in the beauty of the Truckee/North
                                                                 Tahoe region.
                                                                 Imagine a storytelling guide leading a small hiking group
                                                                 in nature to discover a wide-ranging and inclusive series of
                                                                 site-specific performances. From modern dancers gracefully
                                                                 appearing amid an aspen grove, a poet performing under an
                                                                 old growth pine, to a master Washoe basketweaver sharing
                                                                 her basketry, each performance varies immensely.
                                                                 Since 2011, Trails & Vistas has welcomed over 400 third-grade
                                                                 students annually to discover, learn, and create in nature at
                                                                 their Dreaming Tree Field Trips in state parks. Students learn
                                                                 about their outdoor environment directly from members
                                                                 of the Washoe Tribe of Nevada-California, write poems,
