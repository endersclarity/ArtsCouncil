                                                                     For Teens by Teens:
                                                                     The Aspen Collective
                                                                     & Leo Murrell in Truckee
                                                                     In a town with few options for teen entertainment,
                                                                     The Aspen Collective provides a safe sober
                                                                     space for teens by teens, and hosts inclusive
                                                                     concerts focused on harm reduction and
                                                                     mutual aid. The nonprofit was founded
                                                                     in July 2023 by local high school students
                                                                     Eliot Lowe, Leo Murrell and Nico Bolen.

                                                                     At 16, Leo has mastered the art of nonprofit community
                                                                     engagement, producing with co-founders Eliot and
                                                                     Nico Hibernation Fest for Kings Beach Fox Cultural Hall,
                                                                     running a booth at major summer festivals including the
                                                                     Truckee Roundhouse Makers Show, and hosting a Punk
                                                                     Pride Show during Truckee’s first ever Pride Week in 2024.
