                                                     Nevada City and Grass Valley:
                                                     A Dance Lover’s Haven
                                                     Heading over to Nevada City and Grass Valley, the dance
                                                     scene takes on a different, yet equally exciting, flavor.
                                                     One of the highlights of the region is the Dance Because
                                                     Festival. This annual event gathers dancers from across the
                                                     country, offering a wide range of performances, workshops,
                                                     and collaborative opportunities. It’s not just a festival—it’s a
                                                     celebration of movement, community, and shared passion.
                                                     As an artist, participating in this festival has been a deeply
                                                     rewarding experience, allowing me to connect with fellow
                                                     dancers and gain new inspiration for my work.

                                                     Folk dance also thrives in Nevada City,
                                                     thanks to the incredible work of Diana Arbex.
                                                     She has made it her mission to preserve
                                                     and promote the beauty of Brazilian dance,
                                                     drawing upon traditions from various regions
                                                     of Brazil. Her performances and workshops
                                                     not only educate the community about
                                                     folkloric dance forms but also highlight the
                                                     importance of cultural preservation through
                                                     movement. Watching her work has opened
                                                     my eyes to the richness of folk traditions
                                                     and their continued relevance in today’s
                                                     dance landscape.
