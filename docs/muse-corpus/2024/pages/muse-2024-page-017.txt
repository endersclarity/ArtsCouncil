InConcert Sierra (ICS) New Performing
Arts Center
ICS, a nonprofit organization with 78 years of history bringing
stellar classical and choral performances to Nevada County,
has raised millions of dollars to build a 540-seat acoustic
concert hall, a 150-seat black box theater, and a large
conference center in Grass Valley.
The road to the anticipated fall-winter opening of the Crown
Point Events Complex hasn’t always been smooth. In March
2023, within 36 hours of ICS finalizing the purchase of an
existing 42,000-square foot office building with plans to
renovate it, a massive snowstorm caused 14,000 square
feet of the building’s roof to collapse. Without missing a
beat, ICS stayed on course and the new complex is due for
completion in only 15 months and for just under $18 million.
