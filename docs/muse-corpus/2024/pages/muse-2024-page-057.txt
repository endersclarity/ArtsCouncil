                                                              Curious Forge Art
                                                              Center and Makerspace
                                                              13024 Bitney Springs Rd, Nevada City
                                                              thecuriousforge.org

                                                              After fateful trips to Burning Man and
                                                              a Maker Faire in San Mateo in 2011,
                                                              Liam Ellerby and Kara Asilanis founded
                                                              The Curious Forge with the intent to
                                                              cultivate an environment that fosters
                                                              innovation, collaboration, and learning.
                                                              With over 20,000 sq/ft of equipment,
                                                              tools, and a thriving artistic community,
                                                              the Curious Forge offers makerspace
                                                              memberships, art classes, workshops,
                                                              and workforce development programs
                                                              to Nevada County.
