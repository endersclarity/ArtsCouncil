Sunday morning, head to the other side of the tracks. Enjoy a cup of locally
roasted java at Dark Horse. Then walk West River Street and view the historic
buildings, stop in Piper J Gallery where art from the region is exhibited.
Before wrapping up the weekend, take a ride around the Truckee Roundabouts,
where public art graces the roundabouts. Mountain Flowers, created by artist
Troy Corliss offers a whimsical moment. The next roundabout features one of
my favorite installations, the delightful Truckee Ants, forged by metal worker
Matt Parkhurst.
You may be tired after this 48-hour whirlwind, yet Truckee's art, culture,
and beauty will remain embedded in your heart.
