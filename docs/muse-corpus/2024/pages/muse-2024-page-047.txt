Meredith Manning
mmanningworks.com
Meredith lives and paints in Grass Valley, CA where her painterly, abstract
paintings and assemblage pieces are created using acrylics and mixed media
plus found objects. Her love of color, composition and brush technique are
used to evoke unique places and moods that are at once familiar and foreign.
