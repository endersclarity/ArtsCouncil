 Cartoonist R.L. Crabb Celebrates 40 Years
 R.L. Crabb is old-school, perhaps old fashioned as he would phrase it. Still working primarily
   with traditional dip pens and India Ink for the black & white, while coloring his work with
Copic brush pens and in his words “no computer enhancements.” Of course, this comes as no
surprise since he’s been drawing most of his life, although it wasn’t until 1979 that he realized
                             he wanted to be a cartoonist full-time.
