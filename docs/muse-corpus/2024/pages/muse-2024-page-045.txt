                                                                      Acting Up! & Artists-In-Schools
                                                                      Each year NCArts develop lessons that integrate character
                                                                      education themes with California Arts Standards, through
                                                                      which our teaching artists offer weekly Theatre for Change
                                                                      sessions, focused on transformational learning at Bright
                                                                      Futures for Youth and Earle Jamieson Education Options,
                                                                      reaching over 2,000 low-income elementary students.



                                                                      Center for the Arts–CREATE
                                                                      In addition to presenting a robust live performance schedule,
                                                                      The Center for the Arts hosts a series of exciting community
                                                                      focused programs throughout the year to students of all ages.
                                                                      CREATE–The Center Resource for Education, Arts, Theater
                                                                      and Exploration–presents opportunities such as Family Fun
                                                                      Day, summer camps and seasonal classes, student matinee
                                                                      series and Main Stage Playmakers.
