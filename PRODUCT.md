# Product Context

## Register

product

## Product Purpose

The V1 Discovery Map is an internal approval-stage MapLibre alpha for Nevada County Arts Council. Its job is to prove that NCAC can own a culture-forward map experience that feels credible, beautiful, useful, and worth continuing.

The map is the product argument. V1 should help stakeholders react to a concrete cultural discovery experience before the project expands into larger platform, Arts Hub, tourism, or AI-planning debates.

## Audience

- Primary: Arts Council stakeholders reviewing whether the direction is culturally credible and ownable.
- Secondary: visitors and locals who want to discover places, events, and curated cultural routes.
- Tertiary: partner organizations and funders who need to see cultural authority without a heavy platform pitch.

## Product Shape

- Map-first Discovery Map.
- Places are primary.
- Events are a light secondary layer.
- Paths are fixed curated mapped tours with numbered stops and route treatment.
- Detail cards appear after interaction, not as the opening state.

## Non-Goals

- Not an Arts Hub.
- Not a public launch.
- Not a tourism-board platform.
- Not an AI trip planner.
- Not a full calendar/sidebar product.
- Not a public submission or correction workflow.

## Success Criteria

- First load feels NCAC-native and map-first.
- The experience reads as cultural authority, not generic GIS.
- Stakeholders can see places, events, and curated paths working in one alpha.
- Incomplete data is logged, not silently hidden.
- Generated or substitute imagery is visibly labeled as placeholder.

## Anti-References

- OpenDesign static mock as implementation source.
- Old cream/gold/serif atlas direction.
- Generic tourism dashboard.
- Default gray municipal GIS.
- Fake stock imagery pretending to be real place proof.
