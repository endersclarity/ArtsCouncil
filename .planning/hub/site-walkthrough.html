What the Site Does

Events, venues, artists, and editorial content for western Nevada County. 1,969 cultural assets on an interactive map, 198 events from Trumba, 1,650 directory entries, 9 stories adapted from MUSE.

Under the Hood

FeatureDetail
Event pipelineGitHub Actions cron, midnight Pacific daily. Trumba calendar + venue feeds (Crazy Horse, Golden Era, Bodhi Hive). Auto-matches events to venues, auto-deploys to Vercel.
AnalyticsUmami Cloud on all pages. Privacy-friendly, no cookies, no consent banner.
MobileResponsive layout tested at 375px. Haptic feedback on interactive elements. Touch targets 44px minimum.
AccessibilityWCAG 2.1 AA: skip links, focus-visible, aria-labels, reduced motion, contrast, landmarks.
SEOOG/Twitter meta on all pages, sitemap.xml, robots.txt, canonical URLs.
Social shareWeb Share API on mobile (native share sheet), Facebook/X fallback on desktop.
Data1,969 cultural assets (ArcGIS), 211 artists (MUSE OCR), 502 asset images, 198 matched events.

Known Issues

AreaWhat Needs Work
Map UXAsset markers and event venue pins look too similar. The connection between an event and its venue on the map is unclear. What appears at county zoom vs. downtown zoom vs. street level needs tuning. Marker clustering, label priority, and the detail panel need a design pass.

Needs Discussion

TopicContext
Event source partnershipsKVMR, Crazy Horse, Golden Era, and others post their own calendars. The ingestion pipeline exists. We need permission to mirror their listings.
Ticketing integrationCF calls for unified ticketing. What platforms do venues use?
IntegrationCurrently at arts-hub-v2.vercel.app. Options: integrate into the Squarespace site, point a subdomain here, or keep it standalone on Vercel.
Link vs. hostThe About page links out to NCAC programs on nevadacountyarts.org. Should some of that content live on the hub instead?
