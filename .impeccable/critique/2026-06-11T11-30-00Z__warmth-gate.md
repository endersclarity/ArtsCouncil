## In-pass P1 findings — found, fixed surgically, re-verified (cla-67)

1. **[P1→fixed] Red primary buttons failed AA.** "Event details" (charcoal card) and
   "View on map" (place card) were white 13px/800 on `#FF2E00` = 3.72:1 (< 4.5:1, and
   13px bold is not WCAG large text). A contrast regression vs the round-1 "0
   low-contrast" state. **Fix:** scaled both primaries to 19px/800 (WCAG large text →
   3:1 threshold, 3.72:1 passes AA-large), preserving the owner-approved red knockout
   treatment; the brand's own rule ("knockout on red at display sizes") now holds for
   buttons too. Detector re-run: clean.
2. **[P1→fixed, owner-reported] Broken/mis-sized card images.**
   (a) Photos whose URL fails had **no onerror fallback** — cards showed the broken-image
   icon. (b) Placeholder artwork rendered **wildly out of frame**: the HTML
   `height="360"` attribute beat the CSS `aspect-ratio: 16/9` (which only applies when
   height is auto), so every card image rendered 370×360; `object-fit: cover` hid it on
   photos but poster-style category placeholders displayed a broken near-square crop
   ("rts / ganizations"). **Fix:** `height: auto` on `.place-image` (aspect-ratio now
   wins; placeholder verified 380×213.75 = 16:9 desktop, in-frame at 390 mobile, caption
   bar and "Photo not yet sourced" label intact); `onerror` on the place-card photo swaps
   to the category placeholder + label (verified live with a forced 404); event-drawer,
   rail-card, and hint images degrade by removing the broken `<img>` (their layouts have
   photo-less designs). Contract re-ran 10/10 after the fix.
