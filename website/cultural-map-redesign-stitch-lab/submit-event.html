Submit an Event

Help us build a complete cultural calendar for Nevada County

Know about an upcoming event in Nevada County? Submit it here and we'll add it to the cultural calendar. We welcome live music shows, gallery openings, community gatherings, family events, and everything in between.

Submissions are reviewed before they appear on the map. Most events are approved within 24 hours.

What to Submit

  • Live music performances at local venues
  • Art exhibitions, gallery openings, and studio tours
  • Community events, fundraisers, and town gatherings
  • Family and kids activities
  • Recurring events (weekly jams, monthly open mics)

The event submission form will appear here once it has been configured.

Setup required: Create a Google Form with the fields listed in the plan (Event Name, Date, Start Time, End Time, Venue, Event Type, Description, Ticket URL, Image URL, Recurring, Submitter Name, Submitter Email), link it to a Google Sheet, then replace this placeholder with the form's embed iframe URL.
Or fill out the form directly if the embed doesn't load.
