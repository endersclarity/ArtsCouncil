About Nevada County
Arts & Culture

The Nevada County Arts Council is a nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing arts, culture, and creativity across western Nevada County, California. We believe that a thriving creative sector strengthens communities, drives economic vitality, and enriches the quality of life for everyone who lives in and visits our region.

Our work is guided by Culture Forward, a comprehensive strategic action plan developed in partnership with artists, organizations, businesses, and community members. Culture Forward establishes a shared vision for arts and culture through 2030 and identifies six strategic goals: strengthening creative infrastructure, building a digital hub, expanding access to arts education, fostering inclusive cultural participation, developing creative workforce pathways, and amplifying the economic impact of the arts.

This digital hub is the direct implementation of Culture Forward Goal 2: developing a Creative Network Exchange to unify arts resources and showcase the creative sector's community impact. It serves as the central platform for discovering events, exploring cultural assets, and connecting with the creative life of Nevada County.
